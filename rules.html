Please keep hinges, rotors, and pistons to a minimal. Grids causing problems in the error list will be removed. Dont build uneeded things. This means no supermechs or script-heavy machines that arent directly helping your gameplay.






1) Respect your fellow members

Respect your fellow players and engineers on and off game.


2) NSFW

No explicitly racist or sexual Posts, discussions, or profile material of any kind.


3) No spamming chat if you need help

Ping an Admin and we will get with you when we can.


4) NO toxicity!

We will NOT tolerate toxicity! We are a friendly community.


5) Respect your fellow members

No advertising for servers, discords or factions outside of Galactic Conquest. If advertising the server on the official Keen discord, make sure its been 24 hours since the last time ours has been posted.


6) Punishments :)

Admins reserve the rights to punish users at anytime :)


7) Discord ToS and Guidelines

Follow the Discord Terms of Service and Guidelines.


9) 13+

Extending rule 7, any user found to be under the age of 13 will be banned without warning.


10) Respect the staff

Please respect and listen to the very nice staff team.


11) Claim giveaways in 24hrs

All giveaways must be claimed within 24 hours or they will be rerolled, unless stated otherwise.


12) No hydromanning

This means no using your person to board enemy ships or drain their bullets, this must be done with a boarding ship. These rules apply when online and offline raiding, players who have been hydromanned will receive compensation (videos required).


13) No molemanning

This means you cannot use your person to dig around an enemy's defenses in order to get into their base. players who have been molemanned will be compensated (videos required).


14) No exploiting game mechanics

Rotor and piston guns will be deleted on sight, gravity generator weapons and subgrid weapons are allowed however.


15) NAME YOUR FUCKING GRIDS!

Weekly grid clearings will take place, all grids must have the faction tag in the front with a name provided. Name EVERYTHING including subgrids, pistons, hinges, and rotors. Make sure everything is powered as well.


16) Dont be a dick

Don't constantly wipe someone, or if you see someone building up don't attack them just because you can. While it may be fun for your ego, it's not fun for everyone else, dont attack the same person over and over.


17) No insiding/spying

You cannot join a faction for the purpose of gaining information or stealing materials, if you wish to go against your current faction, leave and build up before you do it. Victims of insiding/spying will be fully compensated and the abuser punished.


18) Admin station rules

No grids are to be present in admin station safezones unless for the purpose of trading. Admin stations are not to be blockaded and no pvp is allowed within 5km of the admin stations, and turrets must be off. An antenna will be shown at its location (Main station at [0,0,0])


19) Grid error list

If your grid appears on the grid error list, fix it. Keep the useage of rotors, hinges, and pistons to a MINIMUM. Please turn off all such contraptions as well when not in use.


19) Lost grids are not guarenteed to be replaced

If you lose your ship, dont expect a new one. Compensation in the form of credits or a ship from the shop may be received under extenuating circumstances.
