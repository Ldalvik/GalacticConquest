Grid cleaning and moderation

To keep the server smooth and lag free, scheduled grid cleaning will be done to remove old, destoyed, or inactive grids. Be sure to name ALL your grids, including all ships. Any unnamed grids connected to a named grid may result in losing everything!


Active admins

With multiple active admins in game as well in the discord, there is always someone around to assist you with questions, concerns, or store purchases.


PvP events and giveaways

There will be plenty of chances to test out your favorite gunships and win some extra cash at the same time, as well as random giveaways from time to time. Spend credits at the admin station or buy a new ship!


3 planets

There are 3 main planets you can spawn at. Earthlike, Triton, and Titan. Choose wisely!


32 slot 4.7ghtz Server by ServerBlend

This server has a maximum of 32 players, reaching almost 20-25 on peak days/hours. To accomodate all of this the server runs on a GTX 32GB RAM 4.7ghtz clock. Donations help to fun the next upgrade.


Useful, balanced, QoL mods

With a rich, well-balanced modlist, the server just barely breaks the vanilla experience with Quality of Life mods, and a handful of popular weapon and power mods.
