Faction information

To get access to the #faction-recruitment channel, you must register your faction in the #faction-registration channel. The leader of the faction must be on the discord or have a designated person to take their place.


Server Down

To report when the server is down, type @Server Down. This will alert those who have the ability to restart the server.
